About Us

Tar Heel Roofing and Solar is a locally owned roofing and construction company serving Chapel Hill and the Triangle. After a combined 12 years in the roofing industry, and irritated by dishonest contractors, owners Josh Lawrence and Kadir Ahmedjanov opened up Tar Heel for business. Though our company was officially founded in 2020, our team has a combined 30 years of experience in roofing and exterior home modeling.

Expectations

After calling our office and requesting a quote or consultation, a member of our team will meet with you at your home. We provide free home inspections with a focus on roofing, gutters, and siding. Our goal is to maintain the home, and prevent large expenses such as a roof replacement or full roof installation.

/ Asphalt Roofing

Tar Heel installs asphalt roofing according to GAF’s Weather Stopper Plus Guidelines. That means high quality materials, proper installation and workmanship, and a transferable manufacturer warranty for your roof.

/ Metal Roofing

Popular and long lasting, metal roofs are a great choice for homeowners.

/ Solar

Own your power, and be a part of the new energy revolution.

/ Siding & Gutters

Gutters are a critical part of the home. We offer 5 inch, oversized gutters, which better handle heavy downpours then the typical 4 inch gutter. We have a variety of colors and styles to choose from.

/ Interior/Exterior Painting

Tar Heel works with all major suppliers to make sure you have every available color option.
