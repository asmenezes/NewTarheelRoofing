/ Gutters

Gutters are a critical part of the home. Designed to keep water flowing away from the house, quality gutters will prevent water from backing up under the shingles as well as pooling up around the foundation.
We offer 5 inch oversized gutters, which handle heavy downpours more effectively than a standard 4 inch gutter. We offer a variety of colors and styles. Gutter guards provide additional protection for homes that are surrounded by foliage.

/ Gutter Guards

If your home is surrounded by trees and foliage, you may benefit from gutter guards. These guards prevent debris from accumulating in the gutter, which could cause water to back up and overflow under the shingles, causing ceiling line stains and rotted fascia.
There are many different kinds of gutter guards, ranging from high quality steel mesh to more affordable vinyl and PVC. Different environments callfor different materials, we are here to help you make the best choice for your home.

/ Roof Repair

Tar Heel specializes in repairs of all kinds, whether it be missing shingles, flashing damage, or a leak of unknown origin. Our repair process is as follows:
We first conduct a thorough inspection, being sure to document everything we see. You can follow this documentation live, as we conduct the inspection, via a project link we provide to you. Depending on the issues specific to your home, we will conduct a drone or physical inspection. Post inspection our consultant will discuss in detail everything found. Our goals are simple: to thoroughly document the roof’s condition and to inform and educate you along the way.

/ Roof Replacement

Replacing a roof requires a lot of moving parts. There is the ground crew, responsible for cleaning and dumping debris and trash. Atop the roof are the roofers, as well as the roofing assistants. The assistants manage the continual flow of material, making sure roofers have plenty of nails and shingles. They also catch debris, and pass it down to the ground crew. The roofers, well, they roof.
We know our clients won’t climb the roof to make sure we are completing everything as per the contract. To remedy this, we provide detailed drone photos highlighting key components of the install process. While this givespeace of mind, it also ensures the roof will be covered under the manufacturer's warranty. We offer both GAF and Certainteed warranties, which have a 50 year length, and are transferable if and when you decide to sell your home. GAF and Certainteed are the nation's largest manufacturers of shingles, both have been in business for over one hundred years.
